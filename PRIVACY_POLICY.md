# Privacy Policy

**Workout Timer**

*Last updated: February 10, 2026*

## Overview

Workout Timer ("the App") is developed by Praveen Ravula. This privacy policy explains how the App handles user information.

## Data Collection

The App does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- **No usage analytics** or tracking of any kind
- **No location data** is accessed or collected
- **No cookies or similar technologies** are used
- **No third-party analytics** or advertising SDKs are included
- **No network requests** are made by the App

## Local Data Storage

All workout plans and workout history are stored **locally on your device only** using an on-device database. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

## Permissions

The App does not require any special device permissions.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users. Since no data is collected at all, there are no age-related concerns.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
